NORTHEN VIETNAM

DISCOVER NORTHEN VIETNAM

Experience the charm of Northern Vietnam, a region known for its breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage. From the bustling streets of Hanoi, with its historic temples and vibrant street markets, to the serene beauty of Ha Long Bay with its emerald waters and towering limestone formations, Northern Vietnam offers a diverse array of experiences. Explore traditional villages, savor delicious local cuisine, and immerse yourself in the region's unique traditions and history. Whether you're drawn to natural wonders or cultural landmarks, Northern Vietnam promises an unforgettable journey.

SNAPSHOOTS OF NORTHEN

Northern Vietnam is famous for Ha Long Bay, Sapa, and Trang An. Ha Long Bay features majestic limestone islands, Sapa has terraced rice fields, and Trang An boasts unique caves and waterways.

TASTE OF NORTHEN

Northern Vietnamese cuisine stands out with its light yet flavorful dishes, such as Hanoi's Pho, Bun Cha, and La Vong grilled fish. With four distinct seasons, each one brings its own unique experiences in terms of landscape and climate, adding to the region's distinctive allure.

Pho Ha Noi

Hanoi's Pho is a famous dish in the capital city. The locals are unsure of when it first appeared, but its delicious broth, the chewy texture of the rice noodles, and the fragrant herbs served alongside have made Hanoi pho especially memorable in the eyes of food lovers. Hanoi pho was first known through street vendors carrying it on their shoulder poles. At that time, people commonly referred to it as "pho ganh," which gave the dish its own unique charm. These pho vendors would weave through the streets and alleys, calling out to passersby, adding to the nostalgic beauty of old Hanoi.

Cha Muc Ha long

Cha muc Ha Long is a familiar dish found on both festive tables and everyday meals of the people in Quang Ninh. This local specialty leaves a strong impression on visitors due to its fresh, flavorful taste, distinctive aroma, and the relatively quick and convenient preparation methods. Cha muc Ha Long is not only cherished by the local people but also recognized by both domestic and international visitors. It is a popular souvenir for tourists visiting Ha Long due to its distinctive flavor and assured quality.
