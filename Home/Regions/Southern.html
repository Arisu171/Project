DISCOVER SOUTHERN VIETNAM

Southern Vietnam is a captivating region known for its dynamic cities, fertile landscapes, and diverse cultures. The heart of the region is Ho Chi Minh City, a bustling metropolis where ancient traditions blend seamlessly with modern development. Beyond the urban areas, the Mekong Delta, with its vast network of rivers and floating markets, showcases the agricultural wealth and tranquil beauty of the countryside. From historical landmarks to pristine beaches, Southern Vietnam offers visitors a rich tapestry of experiences, all set against a backdrop of tropical warmth and welcoming locals.

SNAPSHOTS OF SOUTHERN

Southern Vietnam offers diverse beauty, from the lush Mekong Delta to Phu Quoc's beaches. Explore bustling Ho Chi Minh City or the serene countryside for unforgettable experiences.

TASTE OF SOUTHEN

Southern Vietnam's cuisine is vibrant and sweet, featuring coconut milk, fresh herbs, and chili. Popular dishes include crispy pancakes, noodle soup, and caramelized fish in clay pot, reflecting the region’s rich flavors and culture.

Crispy Pancakes

Crispy Pancakes is a beloved Southern Vietnamese dish, known for its crispy, golden pancake filled with shrimp, pork, and bean sprouts. The pancake is made from rice flour and coconut milk, giving it a unique flavor and texture. Typically served with fresh herbs, lettuce, and dipping sauce, Crispy Pancakes offers a perfect balance of savory, crunchy, and refreshing tastes, making it a must-try when exploring the rich culinary traditions of Southern Vietnam.

Hu Tieu

Hu Tieu is a beloved noodle dish from Southern Vietnam, known for its rich, aromatic broth and diverse ingredients. This savory dish features a clear, flavorful soup made from pork bones, served with a variety of toppings such as tender slices of meat, fresh herbs, and crunchy vegetables. The unique blend of textures and flavors makes Hu Tieu a true culinary gem of the South, cherished for its versatility and comforting taste.

Ca Kho To

A beloved classic of Southern Vietnamese cuisine, Ca Kho To is a savory and aromatic dish featuring tender fish braised in a rich, caramelized sauce. The fish is cooked slowly in a clay pot with a blend of ingredients like fish sauce, sugar, garlic, and black pepper, creating a deeply flavorful and comforting meal. This dish reflects the essence of Southern Vietnamese cooking with its harmonious balance of sweet, salty, and umami flavors, often enjoyed with steamed rice and fresh herbs.

Banh Canh Ghe

Experience the vibrant flavors of Southern Vietnam with Banh Canh Ghe , a beloved dish that features tender crab meat paired with thick, chewy noodles in a rich, aromatic broth. This soup is a perfect representation of Southern Vietnamese cuisine, showcasing the region’s love for fresh seafood and bold flavors. Each bowl is a comforting and satisfying experience, offering a taste of Southern hospitality and culinary tradition.
