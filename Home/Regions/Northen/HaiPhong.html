HAI PHONG

Hai Phong, a vibrant port city in northern Vietnam, is known for its bustling harbor, rich history, and dynamic culture. As a major industrial center, it blends modern development with traditional charm. Visitors can enjoy lively markets, colonial architecture, and nearby coastal attractions.

UNMISSABLE ATTRACTIONS
Cat Ba Island
Cat Ba Island, in Ha Long Bay, Quang Ninh, Vietnam, is known for its stunning landscapes. The largest island in the bay, it boasts lush forests, pristine beaches, and dramatic cliffs. Visitors can hike in Cat Ba National Park, explore bays, and enjoy local seafood markets, making it ideal for both relaxation and adventure.
Adress: Cat Ba Island, Cat Hai District, Hai Phong, Vietnam.
Lan Ha Bay
Lan Ha Bay, southeast of Ha Long Bay in Quang Ninh, Vietnam, is known for its serene beauty and clear waters. With limestone formations, secluded beaches, and floating fishing villages, it offers a peaceful alternative to its famous neighbor, ideal for relaxation and exploration.
Adress: Lan Ha Bay, Quang Ninh, Vietnam. Accessible by boat from Cat Ba Island or Hai Phong.
Do Son
Do Son, a coastal district in Hai Phong, Vietnam, is known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant seaside atmosphere. With sandy shores, clear waters, and various attractions, including markets, historic sites, and resorts, Do Son is an ideal spot for relaxation and recreation.
Adress: Do Son District, Hai Phong, Vietnam.
Cai Beo Village
Cai Beo Village, on Cat Ba Island in Ha Long Bay, Vietnam, is a traditional floating fishing village. Known for its scenic beauty and maritime culture, the village features houses and boats on tranquil waters, surrounded by limestone formations. Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood and picturesque bay views.
Adress: Cai Beo Village, Cat Ba Island, Ha Long Bay, Quang Ninh, Vietnam. Accessible by boat from Cat Ba Island.
