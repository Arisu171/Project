DA LAT

Da Lat, nestled in the Central Highlands of Vietnam, is known for its cool climate, lush landscapes, and charming French colonial architecture. Often referred to as the “City of Eternal Spring,” Da Lat features rolling hills, pine forests, and vibrant flower gardens. It’s a popular destination for those seeking relaxation, adventure, or a romantic getaway.

Visitors can explore scenic waterfalls, visit historic sites, and enjoy the city’s rich agricultural offerings, including its famous fruits, flowers, and coffee.

UNMISSABLE ATTRACTIONS
Xuan Huong Lake
Xuan Huong Lake, located in the heart of Da Lat, is a picturesque body of water that serves as a central attraction in this highland city. Surrounded by lush greenery, flower gardens, and pine forests, the lake offers a serene setting for leisurely strolls, boat rides, and picnics. Often called the "heart of Da Lat," Xuan Huong Lake is a popular spot for both locals and visitors, offering beautiful views and a peaceful escape in the midst of the city’s cool, fresh air.
Adress: Xuan Huong Lake, Da Lat City, Lam Dong Province, Vietnam.
Valley of Love
The Valley of Love, located in Da Lat, is one of the city's most romantic and picturesque destinations. Surrounded by lush pine forests and serene lakes, this charming valley is a popular spot for couples and nature lovers alike. Visitors can enjoy scenic walks, boat rides, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills. With its tranquil atmosphere and beautiful landscapes, the Valley of Love is a must-visit for anyone exploring Da Lat.
Adress: Valley of Love, Da Lat City, Lam Đong Province, Vietnam.
Da Lat Flower Garden
Da Lat Flower Garden is a vibrant attraction located in the heart of Da Lat, showcasing the city's famous floral beauty. Spread across 7,000 square meters, the garden features a stunning variety of flowers, from local blooms like hydrangeas and roses to exotic species. Visitors can wander through beautifully landscaped areas, admire colorful flowerbeds, and enjoy the tranquil atmosphere. The garden is a perfect destination for nature lovers and a highlight of Da Lat’s charm.
Adress: Tran Nhan Tong Street, Da Lat City, Lam Dong Province, Vietnam.
Linh Phuoc Pagoda
Linh Phuoc Pagoda, located in Da Lat, is a stunning and unique Buddhist temple renowned for its intricate mosaic design. The pagoda is decorated with millions of pieces of broken glass, porcelain, and ceramic, giving it the nickname "Ve Chai Pagoda." One of its most striking features is the 49-meter-high dragon-shaped mosaic winding through the temple grounds. Linh Phuoc Pagoda is not only a place of worship but also a popular attraction, drawing visitors with its extraordinary architecture and serene atmosphere.
Adress: Linh Phuoc Pagoda, 120 Tu Phuoc, Trai Mat, Da Lat, Vietnam.
