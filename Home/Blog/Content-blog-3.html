Hue: The Ancient Capital and Echoes of Time

Anh Tuan

22 Sep 2024 - 44 min read

Introduction:

Hue, the city of imperial tombs and palaces, preserves the elegant beauty and rich history of Vietnam’s royal past.

Personal Experience:

As I walked through the gates of the Imperial City, I could feel the solemn atmosphere and history of this ancient capital. Tu Duc’s Tomb, with its peaceful setting and unique architecture, was a highlight for me. I also couldn’t resist tasting the famous Bun Bo Hue and simple yet delicious Com Hen

Travel Tip:

Hue is most pleasant in autumn, with cool weather that makes exploring the ancient sites even more enjoyable. Renting a bicycle or motorbike is a great way to get around.

Review:

Hue offers a peaceful and introspective escape into Vietnam’s royal history and culture.
