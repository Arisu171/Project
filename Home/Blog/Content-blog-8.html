Ho Chi Minh City: The City That Never Sleeps and Cultural Corners

Huyen Khanh

09 May 2024 - 6 min read

Introduction:

Ho Chi Minh City, with its vibrant and colorful atmosphere, is a city that blends modernity with a rich cultural past.

Personal Experience:

I spent the day exploring the city, from the iconic Ben Thanh Market, Nguyen Hue Walking Street, to the Notre-Dame Cathedral and Independence Palace. Each corner of Saigon has its own story, and I fully immersed myself in its bustling street food culture.

Travel Tip:

To experience Saigon’s coffee culture, visit a sidewalk cafe and watch the busy world go by.

Review:

Ho Chi Minh City is the ideal place for anyone who loves the energy and excitement of urban life combined with cultural exploration.
