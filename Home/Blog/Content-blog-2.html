Hoi An: A Peaceful Day with Lanterns and Local Delicacies

Linh Chi

21 Oct 2024 - 50 min read

Introduction:

The ancient town of Hoi An, with its quaint streets, mossy roofs, and glowing lanterns at night, offers a serene atmosphere that makes you feel like you’ve stepped back in time.

Personal Experience:

I was captivated by Hoi An’s gentle beauty. Every corner of the town tells a story. I took a lantern-making workshop at a small craft shop and appreciated the delicate artistry involved. Be sure to try the local dishes, like Cao Lau and Che Bap – flavors that linger long after you’ve left.

Travel Tip:

Hoi An is most beautiful at sunset, when the lanterns start to light up. Don’t forget to take a peaceful walk along the Hoai River in the evening.

Review:

Hoi An is the perfect destination for those looking to experience a charming, old-world atmosphere.
