Ninh Binh: A Journey into Nature Amongst Rivers and Mountains

Minh Huong

12 Mar 2024 - 40 min read

Introduction:

Ninh Binh, with its breathtaking scenery of limestone mountains and serene rivers, is the perfect destination for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

Personal Experience:

The boat ride through Tam Coc was one of the most memorable experiences of my trip. The golden rice fields and majestic karst mountains formed a living painting before my eyes. Climbing up to Hang Mua and admiring the panoramic view of Ninh Binh was simply awe-inspiring.

Travel Tip:

Tam Coc is most beautiful during the rice harvest season (May-June). If you’re up for a challenge, hike to the top of Hang Mua for stunning photos.

Review:

Ninh Binh is the perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage, making it a must-visit.
