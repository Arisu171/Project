General requirements when renting a car

General requirements when renting a self-drive car

Include

  • Insurance for car and passengers
  • Vehicle usage time up to 24 hours per rental day

Not included

  • Fuel costs, pick up/drop off outside the centre and insurance claims

Pick up location

  • Free pick up and drop off at airport and in city center

Required documents

  • Original ID/passport, driver's license, personal credit card or family credit card (photo of family card (KK) or marriage certificate must be shared with rental partner prior to pick-up to verify family member's credit card)

Note: Social media accounts and a photo of your ID are also required. If you do not have a social media account, the provider will request additional documents for verification. Learn more in the Terms and Conditions section.
General requirements when renting a car with driver

Include

  • Using the car in the city
  • Rental period up to 12 hours or until 23:59 for each rental day

Not included

  • Fuel, parking fees, road tolls, driver's meal allowance and tips
  • Driver accommodation fee in case of moving out of the central area
  • Using the car outside the city

Pick up location

  • Free pick up and drop off at airport and in city center

The driver will contact you 12 - 24 hours before the pick-up time. In case of same-day rental, the driver will contact you promptly after your booking is confirmed.
